Safe Websites

Learn how to identify safe websites and avoid dangerous ones.

What Is a Trustworthy Website?

A trusthworthy website is a site that provides accurate, safe and reliable information. It protects your personal data and clearly shows who created the material.

Trusted examples include organizations like the World Health Organization, NASA, BBC and more.

How to Spot a Trustworthy Website

  • HTTPS in the URL
  • No spelling mistakes in the web address
  • Clear contact information
  • Updated and well-written content

Look out for:

  • Weird/Suspicious domain names (such as amaz0n-deals.net than amazon.com)
  • Too many pop-up ads
  • "You won a prize!" messages
  • Asking for personal info immediately

💡 Quick Tip

Use a unique password for each important account. If one site is breached, the others stay safe. Consider a password manager to generate and store strong passwords.
