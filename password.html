Module 1: Password Protection

What is password protection and why is it so important?

In the digital era, passwords are the way to protect our account, information and identity online against scammers and theft. And while it may seem wise to use your name, favorite phrase or saying, there are more secure ways to protect our information by following a few simple steps.

The best practices to follow include:

-At least 12 or more characters

-One uppercase Letter

-Use a special character
